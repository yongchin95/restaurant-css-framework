Fuji-san

Fuji-san offers modern authentic Japanese dining in Brussels.

The restaurant is a meeting place where food and drinks are shared with friends and heat, warmth and an all-embracing energy surround.

Most restaurants serve just the dishes you ordered, which you may be used to, but we want to do more with typical Japanese dishes served in an original way!
We want to introduce you to a cuisine that is unknown to you, to share our culinary traditions and our savoir vivre in a warm atmosphere.
